We Are

VIPER KAI!

Located in the heart of the Valley at 1234 Broadway in Reseda, the Viper Kai Dojo lies ready and waiting to turn its students into champions!

Currently we specialize in younger students, ages 7 to 25, but if you are older and interested in the martial arts, please talk to an intstructor to discuss the options.

Viper Kai prides itself on dominating the competition, and we participate in as many events as we can. Click on the video to the right to witness the the awesome Danny, one of Viper Kai's premier instructors, in his amazing last-second triumph at the All-Valley Tournament 30 years ago!

Viper Kai Costs

There are four levels of membership at the Viper Kai Dojo, based on the student's current level of skill

(please click on the "What is Viper Kai?" button below to learn more about skill levels)

The monthly cost of each membership level is listed below:

Basic : $27

Intermediate : $37

Advanced : $47

Expert : $57

In addition, there is a one-time Registration Fee of $15

Students are also expected to purchase and maintain their own uniforms, including belts. Safety equipment, as need, will be provided.

Please speak to an instructor for further details.
