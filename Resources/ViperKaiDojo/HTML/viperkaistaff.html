We Are

VIPER KAI!

Our sensei John is a 65-year-old Vietnam veteran who has traveled all over the world. He is a master of Tang Soo Do, a Korean martial arts based on Karate, which he learned during his years in combat.

Having run a dojo previously many years ago, John left the Valley to find himself in the world. Now he has opened up Viper Kai Dojo, to teach a whole new generation how to dominant and excel in the Viper Kai way.

Our shidoin Danny is a 50-year-old veteran of martial arts. Thirty years ago, Danny won back-to-back martial arts championships right here in the Valley.

Now Danny has a family and owns a chain of car dealerships, but he has kept up with his Goju Ryu Karate mastery throughout the years, and is eager to help new students achive their own excellence in the martial arts.
