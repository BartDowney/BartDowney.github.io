We Are

VIPER KAI!

At Viper Kai, we are committed to providing excellence in our training, and we strive to participate in as many martial arts competitions as we can to highlight that excellence. We also stage many demonstrations and hold promotion ceremonies right here in our own dojo, to give our students a chance to shine for their friends and families. In addition, as part of our ongoing mission to instill responsibility and respect in our students, and our own dedication to making our world a better place, we are part of many community organizations that do important work, such as cleaning up the city and helping to build shelters for the homeless.

See the calendar below for a list of events that are forthcoming for the Viper Kai family. For more information about events, such as time and location, please talk to an instructor.
